Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 • Version 1.2

WeatherRoute: Safer Navigation respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data within our mobile application and website.

1. Location Data

To provide weather forecasts along your exact path, the app may request access to your device location. This data is used to calculate routes and fetch relevant weather information. We do not store your historical location movements on our servers.

2. Third-Party Navigation

If you choose to open a route in third-party apps such as Google Maps or Waze, destination and route data may be shared with those providers according to their own privacy policies.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google may collect and use data such as your device advertising ID to provide relevant ads. You can manage ad personalization in your device settings.

4. GDPR & Your Rights

For users in the European Union, we comply with GDPR. You may request access, correction, or deletion of any personal data where applicable.
